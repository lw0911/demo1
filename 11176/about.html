Company Profile

Jiangsu Hao Yang Lighting Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter refered to as "Jiangsu Hao Yang") was established in February 2012, is a professional LED light source production and export of modern energy-saving environmental protection enterprise. The company is located in Haimen, Jiangsu Province. Haimen is a subsidiary of Nantong. It is adjacent to Shanghai and across the Yangtze River from Shanghai. It is knowns as "North Shanghai" and only 1.5 hours drive from Haimen to Shanghai. It has a long history, a flourishing economy, prosperous science and education. For the survival and development of enterprise, it has provided a fertile soil of well-grounded development! We made total investment of 7 million in USD, covers an area of about 17,000 square meters, the workshop area is of about 13,000 square meters, including an office building and three plants. Our enterprise has 200 employees, with an annual output of various LED lamps 18 millions, the workshop includes two SMD lines , six bulb production lines, one T8 production line, two plug-in lines. The monthly production of bulbs is about 1.5 millions. The annual output value is more than 10 million USD.
